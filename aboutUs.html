Who We Are

Helping Hands Non-Profit was founded in 2015 with the goal of creating a supportive community for individuals and families in need. Over the years, we have grown into a robust organization providing a variety of services, from educational programs to food assistance and volunteer opportunities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where every person has access to the resources and support they need to lead fulfilling lives. We believe in the power of community and are committed to fostering a sense of belonging and cooperation in everything we do.

Our Team

We are a diverse group of individuals united by a shared mission to serve our community. Our team includes professionals, volunteers, and partners who work together to ensure the success of our programs and initiatives.
